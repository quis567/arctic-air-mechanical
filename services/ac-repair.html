Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you get to my home?
We offer same-day service and can typically arrive within 2 hours of your call. For emergency situations, we prioritize getting to you as fast as possible.
Do you repair all AC brands?
Yes, our technicians are trained and equipped to repair all major AC brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Daikin, and more.
What if my AC can't be repaired?
If your system is beyond repair, we'll credit your diagnostic fee toward the cost of a new AC installation. We'll provide honest recommendations and help you choose the best replacement for your home and budget.
Is the diagnostic fee waived if I get the repair?
The $89 diagnostic fee is applied as a credit toward your repair cost. So if your repair totals $300, you'll only pay $211 since the diagnostic fee is already covered.
Do you offer emergency AC repair?
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency AC repair service. When your AC breaks down after hours or on weekends, call us and we'll dispatch a technician to your home as quickly as possible.
