About Arctic Air Mechanical

Family-owned and serving Orlando since 2008.

Built on Trust, Driven by Service

Arctic Air Mechanical was founded in 2008 by Mike Callahan, a third-generation HVAC technician born and raised right here in Orlando. Growing up, Mike learned the trade from his father and grandfather, who taught him that honest work and treating people right were the foundation of any good business.

Mike started Arctic Air with a single truck, a toolbox, and a commitment to doing things the right way. In those early days, he handled every call himself — from emergency repairs in the middle of a July heatwave to routine maintenance on a quiet Saturday morning. Word spread quickly that Mike was someone you could trust in your home.

Today, Arctic Air Mechanical has grown to a team of 12 dedicated employees, but the values haven't changed. Every technician on our team is background-checked, drug-tested, and fully licensed by the state of Florida. We treat every home like it's our own, because we know that when you invite us in, you're placing your trust in us.

We're proud to be a family-owned business in a world of corporate HVAC companies. When you call Arctic Air, you're not getting a call center — you're getting neighbors who care about keeping your family comfortable.
